About Scott Zhou

I grew up from countryside of Shanghai. It tooks me quite a while to see this city growed up and finally became the best city of the world, until I left.

Latter I then went to Guilin (You might had ever heard this beautiful city) for college, receiving a bachelor's degree in Computer Science from Guilin University of Electronic Technology, and met my wife Sophia there.

I am living in Stockholm now with my wife and two kids. I work for H&M in it's AI foundation as a Senior Software Developer Engineer.

In 2005 I founded Pippin Technology in Shanghai, later this company was acquired by UD Technology. At the time in Pippin, we ambition to create a pure software streaming media server based on a general PC platform (no dependency on any DSP chip). We were doing quite well on the technical part but failed badly on the commercial.

Now I'm working for H&M AI foundation team to landing artificial intelligence technologies for this fashion company. AI is not just about playing chess, in such a company as H&M, I got the chance to utilize its huge amount of historical data and make big impacts. I like programming in different languages and try different technology, which keeps me learning and moving.

In my spare time, I love different sports. I always feel very easy to start as a beginner at a new sport but it's very difficult for me to be an expert. I will change my interest very soon after I pass the beginner phase. Maybe I just don't have a talent for sports but it wouldn't stop me to have funs.

You will find a way to get in touch with me on the Contact page. Drop me a mail is always welcome!
